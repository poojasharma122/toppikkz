Adult Only Hotels

Best Adult-Only & Couples Resorts in Las Vegas

For travellers looking to unwind in peace, Las Vegas offers more than just the party scene. From quiet pools and rooftop lounges to luxury suites with spa access, these hotels are tailored for adults seeking a calm, refined experience. Whether you're planning a couples trip, honeymoon, or solo retreat, our Intimate Escapes highlight the best adult-only and couples-friendly stays in Las Vegas.

The Cromwell

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Price per night: $239

  • Adults-only boutique hotel

  • Rooftop Drai’s Beachclub and lounge

  • Elegant, intimate rooms

The Cromwell is small, stylish, and exclusive. It’s one of the few true adults-only hotels on the Strip. Its boutique feel, rooftop nightlife, and luxe interiors make it ideal for couples who want privacy with easy access to Vegas energy.

Nobu Hotel at Caesars Palace

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Price per night: $299

  • Exclusive check-in and priority access

  • Minimalist Japanese design with upscale touches

  • Located within Caesars but separate and private

Nobu Hotel is sleek, discreet, and tailored for guests who want a more serene stay within the heart of the Strip. Rooms are quiet and refined, offering comfort with privacy—ideal for romantic getaways.

Delano Las Vegas at Mandalay Bay

★ ★ ★ ★

Price per night: $219

  • Non-gaming hotel with private lobby

  • Stylish suites with soaking tubs

  • Direct access to Mandalay Bay amenities

Delano blends relaxation with design. The calm, non-casino environment, combined with oversized suites and subtle luxury, makes it a perfect retreat for adults looking for a low-stress experience.

Waldorf Astoria Las Vegas

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Price per night: $329

  • No casino and no smoking throughout

  • Rooftop pool and quiet spa

  • High-end dining and panoramic views

Waldorf Astoria is tailored for travellers who want true calm. Its polished service, spa, and absence of gaming create a peaceful escape right in the centre of the Strip. A strong option for those prioritizing privacy and quality.

  • Boutique-style hotel with private entrance

The Signature at MGM Grand

  • Private, condo-style suites with balconies

The Signature is a calm, off-strip-style stay right next to the action. With multiple towers and no gaming on-site, it’s excellent for couples wanting a blend of space and privacy within walking distance to shows and dining.

Planning an Adults-Only Stay in Vegas?

Not every Vegas trip needs to be loud and crowded. These hotels give you space to breathe and enjoy quality time, with fewer distractions.

Look for non-casino or boutique properties for a quieter atmosphere
Choose resorts with spas, rooftop pools, or in-room soaking tubs
Book early—adult-focused hotels fill up quickly, especially on weekends
