Top Business Hotels & Corporate Resorts in Las Vegas

Las Vegas isn’t just about entertainment—it's one of the busiest conference and trade show cities in the world. If you’re in town for business, you need more than just a place to sleep. You need speed, space, and smart amenities. These Productivity Pads are the best business hotels and corporate resorts in Las Vegas, each selected for their work-ready features and strategic locations.

Vdara Hotel & Spa

★ ★ ★ ★

Price per night: $189

  • Smoke-free and casino-free environment

  • Business centre and in-suite workstations

  • Walkable to Bellagio and Aria

Vdara is the ideal stay for business travellers who want peace and focus. With no casino on-site, it offers a quieter atmosphere just steps from the Strip. The suites are smartly designed for work and rest, and the on-site amenities are geared toward productivity and comfort.
