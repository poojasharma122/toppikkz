Refunds Policy

Top Pikkz is a hotel discovery platform that provides curated information about hotels and resorts in Las Vegas. We do not process bookings, handle payments, or sell accommodations directly through this website toppikkz.com.

Because we are not involved in the transaction process, we do not collect payment from users and do not issue refunds of any kind.

1. No Transactions Are Processed by Top Pikkz

Top Pikkz does not facilitate, manage, or execute any hotel bookings. All bookings made based on information found on our website are completed through third-party platforms or directly with the hotel provider.

We do not act as a travel agency or reservation service, and we do not handle:

  • Credit card transactions

  • Room reservations

  • Cancellations or modifications

  • Payment disputes

2. Refunds Through Hotel Providers or Third Parties

If you have made a hotel reservation through a third-party website (such as a travel booking platform or the hotel’s official website), any request for a refund must be made directly with that third party.

Refunds, cancellation policies, and change fees vary by provider and are subject to their individual terms and conditions.

We encourage all users to review the cancellation and refund policies on the booking platform or hotel website before confirming any reservation.

3. Errors in Listings or Information

While we strive to provide accurate, up-to-date content, Top Pikkz is not responsible for refund claims related to:

  • Incorrect pricing

  • Inaccurate hotel descriptions

  • Mismatched amenities or room features

  • Hotel service experiences

Please verify all details with the hotel or booking platform before completing your reservation.

4. Questions and Concerns

If you believe there is an error in a listing or would like us to review information displayed on our site, feel free to contact us.

📧 info@toppikkz.com

We respond to most inquiries within 1–2 business days.
