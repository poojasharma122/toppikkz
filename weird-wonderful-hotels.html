Weird & Wonderful Hotels

Most Unique and Unusual Hotels in Las Vegas

Las Vegas is a city that thrives on the unexpected. For travellers who want more than a typical hotel stay, these Weird & Wonderful hotels bring personality, eccentricity, and one-of-a-kind experiences to the forefront. From themed rooms and eye-popping décor to surreal amenities and offbeat vibes, these are the most unique hotels in Vegas—guaranteed to leave a lasting impression.

The STRAT Hotel, Casino & Tower

★ ★ ★

Price per night: $79

  • Stay inside an iconic Vegas tower

  • Rooftop thrill rides and 360° city views

  • Retro-modern rooms with bold styling

The STRAT is an icon for a reason. Sleep in the shadow of the SkyPod, then launch into the sky on the Big Shot ride or dine at the revolving Top of the World restaurant. It’s part hotel, part vertical theme park—and it’s unlike anything else in the city.

Luxor Hotel & Casino

★ ★ ★

Price per night: $99

  • Pyramid-shaped hotel with ancient Egypt theme

  • Towering atrium and diagonal elevators

  • Walk-through exhibits and fantasy decor

Luxor’s design alone makes it one of the most visually unforgettable stays in Vegas. Step into a pyramid, ride angled elevators, and immerse yourself in pharaoh-era fantasy with glowing obelisks and mysterious walkways.

View Hotel

AREA15 (Hotel Partner Packages)

Price per night: Varies by partner hotel

  • Access to immersive art experiences at AREA15

  • Interactive exhibits, sensory rooms, and virtual zones

  • Hotels partnered with AREA15 for bundled stays

AREA15 isn’t a hotel—but several hotels partner with this immersive entertainment venue for curated experiences. Expect glow-in-the-dark art, mind-bending installations, and rooms that double as adventures. Ask about AREA15 package deals at nearby hotels.

The Venetian Resort

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Price per night: $279

  • Indoor canals with gondola rides

  • Painted ceilings, piazzas, and Italian opera

  • Themed luxury meets theatrical design

The Venetian may be upscale, but it leans all the way into theatrical luxury. Float through a faux-Venice, admire Renaissance-style murals, and eat under a digital sky that shifts from dawn to dusk. It’s surreal in the best way.

View Hotel

Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino

★ ★ ★ ★

Price per night: $139

  • Movie-themed rooms and memorabilia throughout

  • Star-studded décor and bold neon lighting

  • Unique rooftop pool scene with Strip views

Planet Hollywood turns your hotel into a film set. With celebrity memorabilia, flashy styling, and themed suites, it’s a stay that blends glamour, glitz, and Hollywood quirks.

View Hotel

The Flamingo Las Vegas Hotel & Casino

★ ★ ★

Price per night: $109

  • Retro vibes and neon pink accents

  • On-site flamingo habitat with waterfalls

  • Vintage Vegas charm in a tropical setting

It’s the only hotel in town where you’ll wake up to flamingos outside your window. Flamingo is a throwback to old-school Vegas—with a splash of kitsch, a dash of pink, and a full dose of quirky charm.

View Hotel

The Artisan Hotel Boutique

★ ★ ★

Price per night: $129

  • Art-covered walls, ceilings, and furniture

  • No two rooms are the same

  • Adults-only, eccentric atmosphere

The Artisan is for travellers who want moody, artsy, and totally off-script. Expect mirrored ceilings, surreal paintings, and an anything-goes energy—especially at night.

View Hotel

Madame Tussauds Hotel Experience at The LINQ

Price per night: $149

  • Rooms with wax celeb decor

  • Experience packages with museum access

  • Central location with selfie-worthy settings

Yes, you can “room” with your favourite wax figure. These themed rooms offer fun, fame, and photos. It’s a cheeky, only-in-Vegas experience perfect for travellers who don’t take life too seriously.

Circus Circus Hotel & Casino

★ ★ ★

Price per night: $89

  • In-hotel circus acts and midway games

  • Indoor theme park with roller coasters

  • Perfect for families or nostalgia seekers

It’s loud, chaotic, colourful—and absolutely unforgettable. Circus Circus is part hotel, part carnival, where clowns roam the lobby and acrobats fly overhead. Expect big energy and even bigger stories.

View Hotel

OYO Hotel & Casino

★ ★ ★

Price per night: $59

  • Quirky, off-Strip personality

  • Colorful rooms with kitschy touches

  • Budget-friendly with a party vibe

OYO isn’t polished—but it leans into its reputation with self-aware charm. It’s playful, weird, and slightly chaotic, but if that’s your mood, you’ll love it here.
